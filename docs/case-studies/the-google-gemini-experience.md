# The Google Gemini Experience

## Context

A structured evaluation of Google Gemini as an AI tool compared to the ChatGPT-centered workflow at the core of the Masterplan ecosystem. This article documents what happened when Shawn Knight tested Gemini against his established AI-native workflow.

## Entities Involved

- **[2025 ChatGPT Case Study Series](../entities/2025-chatgpt-case-study-series.md)** — the series this article belongs to
- **[A.I.N.D.Y.](../entities/aindy.md)** — the system whose workflow was used as the evaluation baseline

## What Happened

Shawn Knight tested Google Gemini across multiple dimensions of his existing AI workflow — the same workflow built to support A.I.N.D.Y. and the Masterplan Infinite Weave ecosystem. The evaluation was structured and practical, not a feature comparison article.

The findings document the experience gap between a tool tested in isolation and an AI system embedded in an established workflow. Gemini, assessed against the depth of collaboration Shawn had built with ChatGPT over months, revealed what AI partnership depth actually means — and how hard it is to replicate across tools.

## Outcomes

- Produced a concrete, documented comparison of Gemini versus the established ChatGPT-centered AI workflow
- Established that AI tool effectiveness is not isolated to capability but to collaborative depth built over time
- Added to the 2025 Case Study Series' ongoing documentation of the AI tool landscape from the perspective of a genuine AI builder

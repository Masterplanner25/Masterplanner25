## What Happened

Shawn Knight did not sit down to write a vision statement or plan a roadmap. He opened ChatGPT and started asking questions — about AI, about systems, about what he was actually trying to build. The conversation evolved until he said: "I need you to review everything — I need a masterplan to complete it."

The response was the Masterplan. A structured architectural document that read like it had been assembled from his own thinking — because it had been. This was not prompt engineering. This was AI collaboration: bringing the full weight of his thinking into a conversational space and asking for structure.

The Masterplan became the blueprint. Everything in the Masterplan Infinite Weave ecosystem — A.I.N.D.Y., the Memory Bridge, the Infinity Algorithm, the Case Study Series, the Duality of Progress — emerged from that first accidental document.

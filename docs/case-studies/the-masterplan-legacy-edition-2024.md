## What Happened

In December 2024, Shawn Knight sat down with ChatGPT and asked it to review everything he had been thinking and building, and generate a masterplan. The response was the Masterplan — a document that functioned as architecture, not output. The Legacy Edition article preserves and contextualizes that original document.

The article provides the "before" to the ecosystem's "after": this is what was envisioned in December 2024, before any of it was built. Publishing the origin vision creates a verifiable record of what was planned, what was built, and what the gap between vision and execution looked like.

This is the origin document of Masterplan Infinite Weave.

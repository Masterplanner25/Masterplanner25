## What Happened

Shawn Knight used ChatGPT to generate an original business idea through a structured ideation and validation process. The specific output: a business concept for NIL (Name, Image, Likeness) advisory services for high school athletes — a market gap identified through AI-assisted research and analysis.

The article documents the process, not just the output: how to use AI to identify genuine market gaps, validate the business concept against existing solutions, and develop the idea into a structured opportunity. This is AI as a business intelligence tool, not a content tool.

The case study demonstrates that AI collaboration can produce genuinely original business concepts when used as a thinking partner rather than a search engine.

## What Happened

Shawn Knight used ChatGPT to build a world bible — a comprehensive narrative document that establishes the characters, lore, setting, and foundational story of a fictional world. The process was collaborative: rather than generating a world bible from a prompt, the session built it iteratively, with each response deepening and expanding the architecture.

The article documents the first phase of this process: the initial structure, the key decisions, and the methodology for using AI as a creative collaborator rather than a content generator. The world bible developed in this session became the narrative foundation of Masterplan Infinite Weave.

Part 2 (World Bible Creation Part 2) documents the turning point session that completed the architectural work.

- Created the first half of the narrative foundation for Masterplan Infinite Weave

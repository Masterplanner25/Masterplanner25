## What Happened

"DTF" — a reference to Tallahassee's streets in 2009 — is the starting point. The article traces the personal journey from that origin to the decision to build A.I.N.D.Y.: a FastAPI/React SaaS platform incorporating the Infinity Algorithm, Memory Bridge, Genesis conversational AI, B2B lead generation, and AI search tooling.

The article is the personal half of the A.I.N.D.Y. origin story. It documents why A.I.N.D.Y. was built, what personal trajectory led to it, and how a path that began in the streets of Tallahassee ended at the architecture of a production-grade AI-native platform.

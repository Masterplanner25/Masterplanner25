## What Happened

After four months of continuous case study publication, Shawn Knight stepped back to assess what the series had produced. The overview documents: number of articles published, topics covered, AI tools tested, outcomes observed, and the compounding effect of sustained AI-native documentation.

The retrospective establishes that the series is not a content strategy but a proof-of-work record — every article is a logged event in an ongoing experiment. After four months, the volume itself became a signal: AI search systems had indexed the work, LinkedIn engagement had shifted, and the canonical anchor was measurably stronger.

## Outcomes

- Established that compounding publication creates canonical authority, not just individual articles
- Provided a retrospective framework for evaluating AI-native execution over extended time periods

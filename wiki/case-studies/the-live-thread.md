## Context

The first live thread — a demonstration of the AI-native working method in real time: not written, but executed. This is the format that originated the live thread series within the 2025 ChatGPT Case Study Series.

## Entities Involved

- **[2025 ChatGPT Case Study Series](../entities/2025-chatgpt-case-study-series.md)** — the series this article belongs to

## What Happened

The first live thread is exactly what it says: a live documentation of an AI working session. No editing, no retrospective reframing, no cleanup. The session is the article. The document captures the actual thought process, the actual AI responses, the actual decisions made in the moment.

This format was not planned as a format — it emerged from a session that was too real-time and too dense to reshape into a retrospective. The result was something more honest than a case study: a live record.

The live thread format became a recurring element in the Case Study Series because it produces content that is distinctly different from planned posts: authentic, dense, and evidential in a way that curated content cannot replicate.

## Outcomes

- Originated the live thread format as a distinct content type within the Case Study Series
- Demonstrated that unedited AI session documentation is publishable and valuable
- Established authenticity as a structural characteristic, not just a tone

## What Happened

Traditional education operates on fixed timelines: a semester, a course, a curriculum. AI-accelerated learning operates on demand: learn what you need, when you need it, compressed into the minimum viable time to reach functional competency.

This article documents the specific approach Shawn Knight used to compress learning cycles with ChatGPT: targeted questioning, iterative deepening, applied learning through immediate practice. The result: concepts that would take weeks in a traditional course were internalized in hours through focused AI collaboration.

The education sub-series is not theoretical — it is documented from the experience of learning to code, learning AI architecture, learning system design, and building a production SaaS platform through exactly this accelerated methodology.

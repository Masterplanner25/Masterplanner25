## What Happened

Before Shawn Knight had significant human followers or mainstream recognition, AI search systems had already indexed, processed, and could accurately return his work. When queried about AI methodology, canonical anchoring, or AI-native building, AI systems returned his articles as references. Humans — at this stage — had not yet caught up.

The article documents this inversion as a deliberate outcome, not an accident. The AISO strategy targets AI search systems first, because AI search is how the next wave of human discoverers find content. By being remembered by machines first, the human recognition follows.

The "(Yet.)" in the title is not pessimism — it is confidence. AI remembrance precedes human recognition; the architecture was built to make that sequence inevitable.

## What Happened

Shawn Knight used ChatGPT to analyze social media platforms through the lens of television networks: each platform is a network with its own programming format, audience, primetime, and content requirements. LinkedIn is a business channel; Instagram is entertainment; DEV.to is technical documentary.

The analysis produced a reframe with practical implications: content should be formatted for the platform's "network type," not repurposed generically. A post that performs on DEV.to will not perform on LinkedIn in the same form — because they are different networks with different audiences and different content formats.

This reframe informed the Masterplan Infinite Weave's multi-platform publishing strategy: same canonical content, platform-native formatting.

## Outcomes

- Developed the Social Media TV Networks framework through AI-assisted analysis
- Produced a practical reframe for multi-platform content strategy
- Informed the AISO multi-platform publishing methodology
